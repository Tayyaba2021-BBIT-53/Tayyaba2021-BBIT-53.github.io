Our services

we play a pivotal role in uniting the donors, recipients, and the blood donation center -- a bridge -- making the required transfer and thrive altruism and the social involvement. We offer multiple services which includes: simplifying the donation process, conducting public education and supervise the correct and fair pumping of blood products. A massive part of our work is concerned with educating and informing prospective donors about what we do, how they can help themselves, as well as other people. Through the Internet platform, whose main purpose is, an availability of the knowledge about the eligibility criteria for giving blood, locating a convenient center for donation or a mobile blood drive, and accessing the donation information there. With this information power we allow donors to make knowledgeable decisions, and hence, to engage in the blood donation activity sicceptibly and to dispel myths or misconceptions surrounding blood donation. Conveniently, we offer online registration as well as appointment scheduling. Using above channels donors can build profie, set appointments tah donate and do pre-screening easily from either their computer or telephone. This approach excels not only in elevating donor experience but also enabling blood banks to handle donation reliably and organize effective collection efforts.
